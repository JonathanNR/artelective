Art Class Info.

Taught by Mr Christoph

Art is one of the amazing electives given at Springfield School Jakarta.
It's taught by the one and only Mr Christoph, soon-to-be professional artist, and you can learn how to paint, draw, and even create 3D artwork.

Mr Christoph

A 10-Year Experienced Art Teacher at Springfield School Jakarta

What's your initial dream job?

Professional Artist

What do you do in your job?

I prepare the lessons, do some research, deliver the materials, giving assignments and or tests and last but not least marking

What activities do the students do?

They listen to the lessons, do the assignments that I provide, sometimes they have to do some research and practice in class, and sometimes they have some homework too

What's your favourite artwork done by students?

I love them all, but I especially love those that have a strong message or meaning

Concepts Learned in Class

Styles of Drawing

Making hatching, cross hatching, stippling, contour, cross contour, circles, random lines, and basket/woven using drawing pens of different sizes to create a pattern, scenery or animal. 

Tonal Value


Creating light shades to dark shades using 2B pencils and 6B pencils to make shapes look 3D.

Collage Work


Sketching an idea onto paper, then add colour to it by pasting sliced magazine paper onto it to form a drawing.

Still Drawing/Life


Drawing 3D images of inanimate or dead objects and then colouring it with different tones to make it look realistic.
